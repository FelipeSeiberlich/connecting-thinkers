“Connecting Thinkers” is a website directed at those who like to be on top of the news. The information is provided in a captive way from our debaters in a unique and innovative format. Every week, a new subject is uploaded in the Connecting Thinkers’ Arena, and two professionals with different expertise and background will debate about the same subject.

In the today’s world people is becoming more politicized and speaking their minds as never before. Depending on their beliefs and personal interesting, the communication vehicles can be seriously compromised with partiality. According to Michael Altshuler - an American Author and Speaker about Business leadership - the “Bad news is time flies. The good news is you’re the pilot”.

The way we use our time and the source we select to feed our brains can determine our deepest thoughts and existence and this can influence others positively or not. In the old times for example the Greek philosophers were very well known for their excellent time management skills to grow their knowledge in different areas of actuation as math, sciences, speech, languages, and this mix of understanding made them amazing debaters.
